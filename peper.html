1.0.1Introduction

The SMAT Protocol represents a paradigm shift in the decentralized financial landscape, engineered as a robust Layer 1 blockchain ecosystem. Our mission is to dismantle the barriers between complex blockchain architecture and mainstream financial accessibility. By integrating a high-performance trade engine with a natively secure decentralization server system, SMAT provides an unparalleled infrastructure for both developers and global users. This ecosystem is not merely a platform but a comprehensive financial constitution designed to support direct crypto-to-fiat transactions across more than forty local currencies. We prioritize absolute transparency and mathematical scarcity, evidenced by our fixed supply of 21 million units, mirroring the fundamental principles of sound digital assets. Furthermore, the protocol incorporates a multi-layered security framework, ensuring that every transaction within our CEX, DEX, and gaming arenas remains provably fair and immutable. As we embark on this twelve-year distribution journey, SMAT remains committed to fostering a sustainable, community-driven economy. This document serves as the official legal and technical roadmap, outlining the protocols that will govern the future of the SMAT Network.

Built directly on the SMAT Layer 1 blockchain, the swap engine utilizes an Automated Market Maker (AMM) model to provide constant liquidity for a wide range of digital assets. Whether you are swapping SMAT for stablecoins or participating in yield-generating pairs, the SMAT Swap interface provides a seamless, non-custodial, and transparent trading experience tailored for the next generation of decentralized finance.

3.0.2 SMAT Bridge

The SMAT Bridge is a secure, cross-chain interoperability protocol designed to facilitate the seamless transfer of assets and data between the SMAT Layer 1 blockchain and other major networks. By utilizing a decentralized validator set and a robust lock-and-mint mechanism, the bridge ensures that liquidity can flow freely across fragmented ecosystems without compromising security. This cross-chain architecture is fundamental to our vision of a unified Web3 economy, where the SMAT ecosystem serves as a central hub for multi-chain asset management, empowering users to leverage the best features of different blockchains through a single, secure gateway.

4.0.1 oxSMAT Hedging Power

The oxSMAT Hedging Power is an optional yet potent accelerator designed to maximize mining efficiency within the SMAT ecosystem. By staking oxSMAT, users can trigger a 10x multiplier on their base mining rate, significantly increasing their SMAT coin yield. This mechanism is entirely flexible; users can swap for oxSMAT and stake it at their discretion to boost performance. However, to maintain network stability, a 30-day minimum staking period is implemented. Unstaking within this 30-day window incurs a 1% punishment fee, whereas unstaking after the term is completely free of charge. Throughout the staking period, the mining boost remains active, providing a continuous hedging advantage. Additionally, oxSMAT remains a fully liquid asset, allowing users to buy or sell it on the open market at any time without restrictions, ensuring a balance between high-yield incentives and financial freedom.

5.0.2 SMAT Mainnet Mining Evolution

While our Centralized Exchange (CEX) is being established, the private testing of the native SMAT L1 Blockchain will remain operational. According to our strategic roadmap, the transition to the Mainnet phase represents the most significant chapter of the SMAT ecosystem. Out of the total supply of 21 million SMAT, exactly 57.142% (12,000,000 SMAT) is reserved for the Mainnet Mining Phase, which is scheduled to be distributed over a sustainable 12-year cycle.

Currently, in the Testnet phase, 2,000,000 SMAT have been allocated as rewards. It is important to note that rewards earned during the Testnet phase will initially be issued as SMAT BSC-20 Tokens. As the development of a fully finalized Layer 1 Blockchain is time-intensive, we will first list the BSC20 variant on major exchanges. Once the SMAT L1 Blockchain is finalized, a seamless 1:1 Migration will occur, where every BSC20 token will be converted into Native SMAT Coins.
