The oxSMAT Tokenomics is engineered to create a deflationary, utility-first economic environment. Unlike traditional speculative assets, <b>$SMAT</b> value is anchored to the transaction volume of our proprietary Centralized Exchange (CEX) and the throughput of our native L1 Blockchain. Through a 12-year halving cycle and a unique <b>"Proof-of-Trading"</b> mining mechanism, we ensure that liquidity providers, active traders, and early testnet supporters are mathematically rewarded for their contribution to the ecosystem's growth.
